Data Deletion Instructions

We understand and respect your right to privacy. If you wish to delete your data from our app, please follow the instructions below:

Step 1: Log in to your account

Log in to your account using your username and password. If you have forgotten your password, use the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page to reset it.

Step 2: Go to Account Settings

Once logged in, navigate to the 'Account Settings' section of the app. This is typically located in the dropdown menu under your profile picture.

Step 3: Request Data Deletion

In the 'Account Settings' section, find the 'Request Data Deletion' option. Click on it and confirm your decision in the popup window that appears.

Step 4: Confirm your request

After you have confirmed your decision, you will receive an email to your registered email address. Click on the link in the email to confirm your data deletion request.

Step 5: Completion of Data Deletion

Once you have confirmed your request, we will process it as quickly as possible. Please note that this process may take up to 30 days to complete. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.

If you have any issues or concerns, please contact our support team.
