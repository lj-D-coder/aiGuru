Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I scan questions using QueScan?

Open the app, navigate to the scanning feature, and capture a clear image of the textbook question. QueScan will process the image and provide you with the answer using Generative AI technology.

2. Is QueScan compatible with all textbooks?

QueScan is designed to work with a wide range of textbooks. However, for optimal results, ensure good lighting and capture high-quality images of the questions.

3. How accurate are the answers provided by QueScan?

QueScan strives for accuracy using state-of-the-art Generative AI. While it provides reliable answers, it's always a good practice to cross-check important information for academic purposes.
